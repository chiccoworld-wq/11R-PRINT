Every shirt starts as a blank piece, but the goal is always bigger than ink on fabric. At 11R Print, we care about the process, the details, and the way your brand looks when the order is finished.

From setup to screen, print to cure, every step is done with pride so your shirts feel clean, professional, and ready to represent.

PRINT

Step 1: Underbase

For fabrics like polyester that are prone to dye bleed, we start with a specialized underbase — flooding the screen and pulling the squeegee to seal the shirt and protect the colors printed above it.

Step 2: Blue Layer

Next, blue ink is flooded across the screen and printed with precise, even pressure to build the next layer of the design.

Step 3: Black Detail

Black ink is added last to lock in the fine details and sharp contrast that bring the design to life.

Final Result

The finished print — bold, clean, and ready to wear.
