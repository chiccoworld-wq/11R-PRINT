BEHIND THE PRESS

From bare screen to
finished print.

Every order goes through the same process. No shortcuts. No transfers. Real screen printing — step by step.

01

Screen Setup

The emulsion-burned mesh screen locks into the press. Laser registration lines confirm the design sits exactly where it should before a single drop of ink moves.

02

Flood Coat

Plastisol ink floods the screen, loading every open mesh cell. The squeegee primes the stencil — ready for a clean, full-coverage print stroke.

03

The Print Pull

One controlled stroke. The squeegee forces ink through the mesh stencil and into the fabric — not sitting on top, pressed in. That's what makes it last.

04

Finished Product

Sharp logo, clean lines, bold phone number — printed and cured. Ready to represent on the job site, at the shop, or anywhere the crew shows up.
